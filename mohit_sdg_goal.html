Mapping Business Cases to SDG Targets and Indicators

Target 11.3.1: Ensuring sustainable land use for urban-rural integration.

Explanation: Agriculture promotes sustainable food production and rural development in harmony with urban expansion.

Target 11.5.1: Reduce disaster-related impacts, including water management.

Explanation: Dams contribute to sustainable water supply and flood control while ensuring climate resilience.

Target 11.2.1: Access to safe, affordable housing and transportation.

Explanation: Residential areas need proper planning for housing, infrastructure, and transport systems.

Refined Business Cases Based on SDGs

  • Sustainable Agricultural Practices: Eco-friendly farming techniques, organic farming, and use of renewable resources.
  • Land Management: Efficient land use, integrating agricultural activities with urban planning to ensure balanced development.
  • Food Security: Promoting local food production and reducing food waste.
  • Climate Resilience: Developing adaptive farming techniques to address climate challenges.
  • Water Management: Sustainable water use through proper storage and distribution systems.
  • Flood Control: Dams prevent floods and reduce risk during heavy rains, thus ensuring safety for urban and rural populations.
  • Energy Generation: Dams contribute to renewable energy production through hydropower generation.
  • Climate Adaptation: Ensuring long-term water resource sustainability in the face of climate change.
  • Affordable Housing: Ensuring that housing is accessible to all income groups.
  • Sustainable Urban Design: Planning for energy efficiency, waste management, and green spaces.
  • Public Transport: Developing sustainable and affordable transportation systems to reduce traffic and pollution.
  • Inclusive Urban Development: Creating residential areas that cater to diverse populations and provide equal access to services.

Refined Business Cases Based on SDGs

  • Wastewater Treatment: Improve sewage treatment to prevent pollution and enhance public health.
  • Recycling Water: Implement systems to recycle treated wastewater for use in non-potable applications.
  • Efficient Waste Collection: Ensuring regular and adequate collection of urban solid waste to reduce environmental impact.
  • Green Infrastructure: Integrate green infrastructure into sewage treatment plants to enhance sustainability.
  • Waste Reduction: Promote recycling programs to reduce waste sent to landfills.
  • Public Awareness: Educate communities about the importance of waste segregation and recycling.
  • Recycling Facilities: Establish efficient recycling centers to manage urban solid waste.
  • Sustainable Urban Living: Promote a circular economy by reusing and recycling materials within cities.
  • Safe Public Spaces: Improve security in public spaces to enhance accessibility and inclusivity.
  • Public Safety Programs: Implement initiatives to reduce harassment and crime in urban areas.
  • Inclusive Infrastructure: Ensure that police stations and public safety infrastructure are accessible to all, including persons with disabilities.
  • Community Engagement: Involve the community in maintaining safety and security within public spaces.
